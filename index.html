WHO WE ARE

Our Story

From a Small Workshop to a Global Textile Bridge

Laj was founded in Faisalabad, Pakistan’s textile capital, with a simple but powerful vision — to take quality craftsmanship beyond local markets and build a global textile bridge. What started as a small sourcing setup has grown into a structured and reliable procurement network that now connects certified manufacturers, export houses, and international retail brands across more than 25 countries.

Over time, we have developed a fully integrated supply ecosystem that includes certified mills, vertically aligned stitching units, and compliant dyeing facilities operating under globally recognized standards such as OEKO-TEX®, BSCI, and GOTS-aligned production systems. Every shipment is handled with strict attention to detail, undergoing AQL 2.5 quality inspections, third-party verification when required (including SGS testing), and complete export documentation such as packing lists, compliance certificates, and lab reports.

Today, our sourcing capabilities extend across home textiles, apparel, denim, institutional fabrics, and custom OEM manufacturing. With a coordinated vendor network, we manage over 500,000 export-ready orders annually while maintaining consistency, compliance, and reliability at every stage of production and delivery.
