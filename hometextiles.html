THERMAL BLANKET DIVISION

Premium Thermal Blanket Solutions

From lightweight thermal layers to heavy winter blankets, LAJ delivers premium blanket solutions engineered for warmth, comfort, and durability. Designed for residential, Institutional, and export markets, our blankets ensure superior insulation and long-lasting performance in cold climates.

Cotton Cellular Thermal Blankets

Year-round comfort with one intelligent blanket — our 100% Cotton Cellular Thermal Blankets feature a lightweight, open-weave design that provides breathable coziness on cooler summer nights and excellent thermal insulation during cold winters.

Built to last through constant use — made from soft, dense cotton, these blankets retain their original texture, color, and structural integrity even after repeated industrial laundering. No fading, no pilling, no loss of quality.

Trusted in hospitals and healthcare facilities — clinicians choose our blankets to keep patients warm and comfortable because they are gentle on sensitive skin, easy to sanitize, and provide consistent thermal regulation.

Elegant enough for premium settings — beyond functionality, our blankets add a stylish, luxurious finishing touch to hotel guest rooms, resorts, assisted living centers, and high-end residential interiors.

Thermal Fleece Blankets

Consistent warmth in any climate — as leading exporters of Thermal Fleece Blankets, we source synthetic blankets using 100% virgin fibers such as polyester and acrylic. These materials ensure durability, softness, and reliable thermal performance.

Certified and standardized — our blankets are available in low, medium, and high thermal ratings, each backed by international quality standards and certified TOG testing.

Choose the right warmth level for your needs:

  • • Low Thermal Fleece Blanket (TOG 1.5) – Ideal for indoor use in rooms with heating facilities, or for outdoor use in milder conditions. Lightweight and breathable.
  • • Medium Thermal Fleece Blanket (TOG 2.5) – A versatile all-season option, suitable for both warmer and cooler conditions. Balances insulation with breathability for year-round comfort.
  • • High Thermal Fleece Blanket (TOG 4) – Designed specifically for cold climates. Provides maximum warmth and heat retention — best for freezing temperatures, unheated spaces, or winter travel.
