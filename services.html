Vendor Selection

Every sourcing outcome starts with choosing the right manufacturing partner.

We apply a requirement-first filtering model before any vendor is approached.

Choosing the wrong vendor is the most expensive mistake in sourcing — and the hardest to recover from. We treat vendor selection as a structured due diligence process, not a directory search. Before any outreach begins, we define your full requirement profile: product specifications, quality benchmarks, required certifications (OEKO TEX, BSCI, Sedex, GOTS), target volume, and acceptable cost range. Every potential vendor is assessed against this profile — factory audits, compliance records, capacity verification, and sample history are reviewed before any engagement begins.

Merchandising & Design

We turn buyer briefs into precise, factory-ready technical packs.

Covering home textiles and apparel from concept through approved sample.

Vague specifications are the root cause of most sampling failures. Our merchandising function exists to eliminate that ambiguity. We translate your product vision — whether a mood board, a reference sample, or a written brief — into a detailed technical pack that factories can execute without guesswork. This covers material composition, construction method, stitch density, colorway specifications, tolerance limits, and packaging requirements across home textiles and apparel.

Market Research

Ongoing intelligence across pricing, raw materials, and supplier landscape.

Coverage across 7+ global trade fairs and leading forecast agencies.

Sourcing decisions made without current market data are guesses. We maintain a live intelligence function that tracks raw material pricing, supplier capacity shifts, freight rate movements, and regulatory updates across home textiles and apparel. Our coverage spans Heimtextil, Première Vision, Texworld, and Canton Fair, alongside WGSN, Just-Style, raw material exchanges, and on-ground supplier data from Pakistan's core textile manufacturing hubs.

Production Follow Up

Milestone-by-milestone tracking from raw material to container loading.

ERP-linked real-time status with proactive delay alerts.

Production delays are rarely sudden — they build up quietly across missed checkpoints. We prevent that through structured, milestone-based follow up at every stage: raw material receipt, cutting, stitching, finishing, packing, and container loading. Inline quality checks are conducted during production so defects are caught early. Our ERP-integrated tracking system provides real-time status against your agreed timeline, with proactive alerts whenever a milestone is at risk.

Quality Assurance

Four-stage inspection framework applied across every order.

AQL 2.5 enforcement with optional SGS third-party validation.

Quality cannot be inspected into a product at the end — it has to be built in at every stage. Our assurance framework runs four layers: pre-production inspection, inline inspection during stitching and finishing, final inspection enforcing AQL 2.5 standards with a maximum 4% defect tolerance, and pre-shipment verification before the container is sealed. For orders requiring independent validation, we coordinate SGS third-party testing — providing audit-ready documentation and full buyer confidence.

Shipping & Logistics

Ocean, air, and multi-modal freight under FOB and CNF terms.

Complete export documentation handled in-house.

A perfect product held up in customs erases every advantage gained upstream. We manage the full logistics cycle. Freight options cover FCL and LCL ocean shipments, air freight, and multi-modal combinations under FOB or CNF trade terms. All export documentation is handled in-house: Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin, commercial invoices, packing lists, and export declarations — accurate, compliant, and ready for customs clearance at destination. Real-time tracking provides visibility from factory gate to your warehouse door.

After Sales Service

Active monitoring from dispatch through warehouse receipt.

Structured feedback loop that improves every subsequent order.

Our engagement doesn't end when the container leaves. We monitor arrival, customs clearance, and warehouse receipt — confirming the order landed as specified. Any discrepancy in quantity, quality, or documentation triggers an immediate investigation with a fast, documented resolution. Every completed order feeds into a structured review that drives continuous improvement. Performance data, return rates, and buyer feedback are fed back into your sourcing strategy so each order becomes more efficient than the last.

Supply Chain Integration

All sourcing functions connected under one unified data architecture.

After-sales insights looped back into vendor and quality planning.

Most sourcing problems are coordination failures — functions operating in silos with no shared data. Our supply chain integration model eliminates that. A single data architecture connects vendor selection, design, production, quality, logistics, and after-sales into one traceable workflow. ERP-tracked milestones provide real-time visibility across the full lifecycle. Lessons from after-sales reviews are systematically fed back into vendor evaluation and quality planning — creating a closed improvement loop with one partner, one standard, and complete visibility.
