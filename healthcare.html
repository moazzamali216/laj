Doctor & Surgical Wear
Precision Engineered for Healthcare Professionals

Our doctor and surgical wear is developed for environments where hygiene, safety, and performance are non-negotiable. Through advanced fabric engineering, we deliver garments that ensure durability, all-day comfort, and compliance with international medical standards.

Every garment is constructed using hospital-grade textile systems optimized for infection prevention, extended wear comfort, and repeated industrial laundering cycles.

• Fabric composition: 55% recycled polyester / 45% combed cotton (poplin weave) – balanced strength and breathability

• Fabric weight: 165 GSM – engineered for repeated industrial wash cycles

• Performance finish: Anti-microbial + fluid-repellent coating meeting hospital hygiene standards

• Construction: Poly-cotton core thread seams designed for skin-safe long-shift wear
